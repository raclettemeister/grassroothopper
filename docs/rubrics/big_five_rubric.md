# Big Five Rubric

## Purpose

Lightweight, non-clinical guidance for summarizing self-report and inferred Big Five signals.

## Guardrails

- not diagnostic
- not a hiring truth machine
- used mainly for interview tailoring and fit discussion
- do not auto-reject based on inferred personality
